1. Subscriptions

LogiBrisk operates on a SaaS (Software as a Service) model. We offer both monthly and annual subscription plans. Your subscription will automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle.

2. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time by contacting your dedicated account manager or emailing support@logibrisk.com. You must submit your cancellation request at least 7 days before your next billing date to avoid being charged for the next cycle.

Upon cancellation, you will continue to have access to the platform until the end of your current paid billing period.

3. Refunds

Monthly Plans: We do not offer refunds or credits for partial months of service, downgrade refunds, or refunds for months unused with an open account.

Annual Plans: If you cancel an annual plan within the first 30 days of the subscription period, you are eligible for a prorated refund for the remaining 11 months. After 30 days, annual subscriptions are non-refundable.

4. Implementation Fees

Any one-time setup, data migration, or custom integration fees charged during the onboarding process are strictly non-refundable once the implementation work has commenced.
