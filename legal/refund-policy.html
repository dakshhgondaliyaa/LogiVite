At LogiBrisk, customer satisfaction is important to us.

1. Free Trial

We offer a free trial without collecting payment details. Users are encouraged to evaluate the platform before subscribing.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refund requests must be made within 7 days of subscription start date. Requests beyond this period will not be accepted.

3. Refund Process

Submit refund request via email with account details. Upon approval, refunds will be processed within 5 to 7 working days.

4. Mode of Refund

Refunds will be credited to the original payment method.

5. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Requests after 7 days
  • Partial usage beyond eligibility window
  • Change of mind after significant usage

6. GST Treatment

GST portion, if applicable, will be handled as per Indian tax regulations.

7. Working Days

Refund processing occurs on Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays.
