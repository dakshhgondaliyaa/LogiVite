Frequently Asked Questions

Does Logibrisk supply transport management software in India?

Yes, Logibrisk is a leading transport management software provider in India, offering complete solutions for fleet, FTL, PTL, and transport management tailored to Indian logistics businesses.

What features does Logibrisk's fleet management software offer?

Logibrisk offers advanced fleet management software with features like real-time vehicle tracking, fuel management, driver behavior monitoring, and automated maintenance scheduling to improve operational efficiency.

Can Logibrisk's TMS handle both FTL and PTL operations?

Absolutely! Logibrisk provides specialized software for both Full Truckload (FTL) and Part Truckload (PTL), helping transporters manage dispatches, billing, and routing smoothly across operations.

How can Logibrisk transport management software help reduce costs?

Our transportation management software helps reduce fuel wastage, prevent unauthorized vehicle use, optimize delivery routes, and automate manual processes, which significantly lowers overall operational costs.

Do you provide a driver app with your fleet management software?

Yes, Logibrisk's fleet management software includes a dedicated driver app that allows drivers to update trip statuses, upload PODs (Proof of Delivery), and manage expenses directly from their smartphones.

Is Logibrisk's logistics software suitable for small transport businesses?

Yes! Logibrisk's logistics software is scalable and designed to meet the needs of all business sizes, from small fleet owners to large logistics enterprises, ensuring easy implementation and growth.
