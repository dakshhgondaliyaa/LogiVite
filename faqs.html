Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the platform and how it works.

General Platform

What is LogiVite SaaS?

LogiVite SaaS is our flagship Transport Management System designed specifically for Indian logistics companies. It handles FTL, PTL, Fleet Management, and automated billing in a single cloud-based dashboard.

Do I need an IT team to manage this?

Not at all. LogiBrisk is a fully managed cloud service. We handle all hosting, server maintenance, data backups, and security updates. You just log in via your browser and manage your business.

Does it work on mobile phones?

Yes. The admin dashboard is fully responsive and can be accessed on tablets or phones. Furthermore, we provide a dedicated Android app for your drivers to view trips, upload PODs, and manage expenses.

Pricing & Billing

How is the software priced?

We charge a flat monthly or annual subscription fee based on your fleet size and the modules you need. There are no hidden charges, and you don't pay per-user fees.

Are there any setup fees?

Standard setup is free. If you require extensive data migration from an old legacy system or custom API integrations, a one-time implementation fee may apply. This will be discussed upfront before you sign anything.

Technical & Integrations

Can it integrate with Tally?

Yes, we offer seamless integration with Tally ERP 9 and Tally Prime. Invoices and vouchers generated in LogiBrisk are automatically pushed to Tally, eliminating double entry.

Do you integrate with FASTag and GPS?

Yes. We pull data from major GPS providers and FASTag toll APIs so you can cross-verify trip routes and toll deductions directly within our dashboard.

Is my data secure?

We use enterprise-grade AWS servers located in Mumbai to ensure data residency compliance. All connections use 256-bit SSL encryption, and databases are backed up daily.

Onboarding & Support

How long does onboarding take?

Typically 5 working days. This includes setting up your account, configuring your rate charts, migrating vehicle/driver master data, and conducting a training session with your staff.

What kind of support do you provide?

We provide phone, email, and WhatsApp support during business hours (9AM - 6PM IST). Critical server issues are monitored 24/7.
