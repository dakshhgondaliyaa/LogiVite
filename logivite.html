The Workflow

From booking to billing in 4 simple steps.

1. Create Booking

Enter origin, destination, and freight details. E-Way bill is generated instantly via API.

2. Assign Trip

Select vehicle and driver. Driver receives trip details and advance payment on their app.

3. Track & Deliver

Monitor live location. Driver uploads digital POD upon unloading.

4. Auto Billing

System auto-calculates final freight and generates GST invoice immediately.
