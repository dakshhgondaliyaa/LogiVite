What is LogiVite?

LogiVite is a lightweight and user-friendly transport management platform designed specifically for small to mid-sized transportation businesses. It helps transporters manage bookings, PODs, E-Way Bills, billing, and delivery operations from one centralized platform without depending on Excel sheets, paperwork, or constant manual coordination.

Built as the lite version of LogiBrisk TMS, LogiVite simplifies daily transport operations by improving real-time visibility, reducing manual work, and helping teams operate more efficiently with smarter digital workflows. From booking to delivery, everything stays organized, connected, and accessible in one place, so your business runs smarter, stays organized, and grows without the chaos.

For small and mid-sized transport businesses looking to take their first serious step toward transport business digitization India has been waiting for LogiVite, which is exactly where that journey begins.

Still Running Your Transport Business the Hard Way?

If any of these sound familiar, you are not alone, and you are losing more than you realise. Every manual process in your business is quietly costing you time, money, and growth.

Endless Excel Chaos

Every booking, trip update, and delivery is tracked in a spreadsheet. One wrong entry breaks everything, and finding that error costs you hours you do not have.

Delayed POD Collection

Your driver delivers the goods, but the paper proof reaches your office days later. Without Digital POD Software, billing stays stuck, cash stays locked, and customers stay uninformed.

Manual E-Way Bill Hassles

Your team spends hours every day generating, extending, and tracking E-Way Bills by hand. One missed expiry means a penalty your business should never have to pay.

Constant Driver Follow-Ups

Your office team calls drivers multiple times a day just to know where a truck is. No live tracking, no real-time updates, just guesswork and phone calls that slow everyone down.

Billing Errors That Hurt Revenue

Manual freight calculations lead to wrong invoices, customer disputes, and payments that get delayed or never come at all. Small errors quietly drain your revenue every month.

No Visibility Into Business

Which route is profitable? Which customer delays payment? Which driver performs best? Without data, every business decision is just a guess.

How LogiVite Works

1

Create Booking

No more filling registers or updating Excel sheets. Add your consignment details, customer information, and trip data in minutes directly from LogiVite. Everything is saved, organized, and accessible anytime.

2

Assign Trips & Drivers

Allocate the right vehicle and driver in seconds. Once assigned, track every trip's progress in real time, no need to call drivers for updates or guess where your consignment is.

3

Upload PODs & Track Deliveries

Your driver reaches the destination, captures the delivery proof right from the mobile app, and uploads it instantly. Your office sees it live. No paper POD. No delay. No missing proof at billing time.

4

Generate Billing & E-Way Bills

The moment POD is confirmed, LogiVite automatically generates a GST-compliant invoice and sends it to your customer. No manual billing. No delays. Your payment cycle starts the same day delivery happens.
